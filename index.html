about me

MENU

Fascinated by the way web products can influence perception, shape opinions and create feelings, I decided to pursue the journey of web programming with a current focus on front-end developing.

I have found great joy in creating eye-pleasing, interactive and intuitive websites, as well as web and mobile apps.

Curiosity is my biggest trait as well as a flaw. It is what drives me to be endlessly commited to my work, or boring about it, depending on who you ask.

As a self-taugh developer, perseverance and adaptivness are right there next to programming as the things I learnt in the last few years.

I consider developing a skill never completely learnt, thankfully that aligns with my unfullfing need to constantly learn, explore and improve.

Altough I am currently focused on front-end developing, when I close my eyes the sight of becoming a full-stack developer is more clear every day. One could say I took the quote "A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better than a master of one" quite personally.
